About this property

This beautifully presented semi-detached family home is situated on one of Isleworth's most sought-after residential roads. Having undergone a complete architectural renovation, the property combines traditional character with modern efficiency.

The ground floor features a bright, open-plan reception leading into a bespoke kitchen-diner with premium integrated appliances and oversized bifold doors that open onto a landscaped south-facing garden. High ceilings and engineered oak flooring run throughout the living spaces.

Upstairs, three generous double bedrooms offer ample storage, with the master benefiting from a sleek en-suite shower room. The property is ideally positioned for commuters, being within walking distance of Isleworth station.
