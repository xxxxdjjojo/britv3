The Lifestyle

Life in Kensington revolves around refined simplicity. Morning jogs through Kensington Gardens are followed by coffee on Church Street. It is an area that values privacy as much as it does proximity to world-class retail on the High Street.

Cultural institutions like the V&A, Royal Albert Hall, and the Natural History Museum provide an intellectual backdrop that few other global neighborhoods can match.
