Auth Layout Structure

Which layout should the auth pages use? This affects every page in the flow (login, signup, forgot password, etc.)

B — Split Panel Stitch Design

Form on the left (45%), property image panel on the right (55%). Premium feel matching the Stitch screens. Desktop only — collapses to centered card on mobile. FAANG standard.

A — Centered Card Current

Current implementation. Card centered on neutral-50 background. Simpler to implement, consistent across all screen sizes. Clean but less premium.

C — Full Immersive

Property image fills the entire screen, card floats over it with a frosted/translucent treatment. Most dramatic visually but adds complexity and can hurt readability.

Recommendation: Option B (Split Panel) — it matches the Stitch designs exactly, is the gold standard for premium auth flows (Notion, Linear, Vercel all use it), and works beautifully on the property imagery Britestate has.
